# Anglophone-vs.-Non-Anglophone-Movies-Comparisons-Within-and-Between

Content: This programs is a film market analysis based on the IMDb dataset.


Introduction: With an increasingly interconnected world, some movies exist in multiple languages so that they could be distributed in different nations across the world. However, with being shown in various countries with unique cultures and varying values, a question arises: does the language and region that a movie is in affect its reception? Using the IMDB dataset that includes thousands of movies and their data, our group wants to answer this question by analyzing variables such as language, region, and genre. In the contemporary use of the data, we must acknowledge that the predictions and patterns that we may find aren’t all-encompassing or consistently accurate; as we know, society has changed over time, and so the findings we uncover in this age may not be wholly applicable to a past time, or even the future.
